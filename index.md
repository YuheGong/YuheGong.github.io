When writing your proposal, keep the following questions in mind: 

    Is this a specific and focused area of research? 

    Can I measure the success of this project in clear ways?

    Is this achievable in the timeframe of 3-4 years? 

When reading the proposal, the following things will be considered

    Does the candidate have a clear reason and motivation to apply to this programme? 

    Do their interests match well with that of their proposed supervisors? 

    Is this an original or novel project? 

    Do they have a good understanding of the current research related to their project? 

    Do they have the prerequisite knowledge of research techniques? 

    Would they be able to undergo extended and intense research?
